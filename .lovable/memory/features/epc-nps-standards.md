- PMS and Pipe Schedule module use EPC-preferred NPS only: ¼" to 24" excluding 2½" and 3½" (procurement issues)
- Schedule recommendation engine prioritizes SAFETY over cost/weight: target utilization 50-75%, >85% flagged
- Small bore (≤ 1½") minimum Sch 80 mandatory per EPC practice (threading/handling damage)
- Materials management: prefer fewer schedule varieties across a spec
- PMS passes pre-normalized SI values to selectPipeSchedule with unitSystem "SI" to avoid double conversion
